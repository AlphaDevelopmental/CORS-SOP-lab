# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. This applies to all community members, contributors, and participants.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Welcoming feedback and new ideas
- Focusing on constructive criticism
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences
- Assuming good intent

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination (based on any characteristic)
- Hostile or demeaning language
- Unwelcome sexual or romantic advances
- Doxing or sharing private information
- Any form of abuse or bullying

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Code discussions
- Any official project communication

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Report it to the project maintainers
2. Include details of the incident
3. Be respectful but clear

All reports will be reviewed confidentially. We are committed to maintaining a safe and welcoming community.
